I Keep Coming Back to Stardew Valley


Stardew Valley is a simple game about inheriting a farm. You basically get to play the game anyway you want to play. The game came out in 2016, and even though I have other games to play (I still haven't finished Devel May Cry 5) I keep coming back to it. If I had to say why I guess I would have to be the life I have in the game. As weird as that sounds I genuinely care about my fictional farm and want to take care of it. I want the people in the fictional town to do well and want them to like me. I want to explore the mines and fish everywhere. It's a form of escapism for me I guess. I can leave my normal life for this fictional life where I have other people to take care of and people to help. I don't think I'll ever stop playing this game and honestly I look forward to see the state of it in a couple years.
