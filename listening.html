Black Metal


Blues, gospel, and soul meets harsher music are the words you see when you look up Zeal & Ardor. Those words are extremely accurate. The music is a combination of negro spirituals and black metal. That is a combination you wouldn't expect to work but it does and it's amazing. With only two albums out this band is still fairly new but I can't recommend them enough.
